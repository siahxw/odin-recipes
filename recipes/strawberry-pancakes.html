Strawberry Pancakes

Description

Cinnamon pancakes with strawberry sauce.

Ingredients

    Pancakes:

  • 1 cup self-rising flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp. sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Low-fat sour cream for the pancakes

    • Strawberry sauce:

  • 4 cups fresh strawberries
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp. vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
  • 1 1/2 tsp. cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup water

Steps

    Pancakes:

  1. Combine all of the above in a bowl and mix.
  2. Heat a large cast-iron griddle with a pat of butter. Ladle the batter onto the griddle.
  3. When little holes in the pancake form; flip and finish cooking.

    4. Strawberry sauce:

  4. Combine all of the above in a saucepan and simmer on low until thickened.
  5. Serve with the pancakes.
