Jamaican Curry Chicken

Description

Spicy chicken seasoned well with veggies in a curry sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp Veg. oil
  • 1 lrg Onion, sliced
  • 1/2 x Bell pepper, diced
  • 2 x Cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 x Baby tomatoes
  • 3 stalk green onions, minced Thyme to taste Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 dsh All-purpose seasoning
  • 1 dsh Curry pwdr
  • 1 whl chicken, cut in small pcs

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a saucepan and add in garlic.
  2. Combine the rest of the ingredients in a separate bowl and add in chicken.
  3. Once garlic is heated, add in chicken mix to saucepan. Cover and steam till cooked.
